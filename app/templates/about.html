About Us

Welcome to the Travel Planning Platform! We help travelers organize their journeys, save beautiful memories, and share experiences with others.

This platform was developed by a passionate team as part of a university project. It integrates Flask, Bootstrap, and modern web technologies to provide a seamless experience for trip planning and memory keeping.

Features include:

  • Interactive travel plans with map integration
  • Photo-based memory galleries
  • User accounts and profiles
  • Secure login and session handling

Thank you for using our platform. We hope it becomes your favorite travel companion!
